About Me

I am 27 years old and I have IPF (Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis) I found out I have IPF when I was 21. At first, I did not think about what this illness will do to me nor did I cared. As the illness progresses, I need oxygen and more help around the house. I found that it is not a death sentence but an inconvenience. I am still able to travel and explore the world. Join me in my journey and I will try to show you that nothing stops you from doing everyday stuff.
